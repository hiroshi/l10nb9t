l10nb9t - The localization bookmarklet

What?

Just click this bookmarklet link. You may see date time representations in anohter time zones are converted to your local timezone (depend on your system settings).

Examples

  • Sep 4, 2013 18:00 UTC
  • Sep 10, 2013 10:00 PDT
  • Sep 10, 2013 10:00 PT
  • Sep 11, 2013, 4PM - 6PM US/Pacific
  • Nov 11, 2013, 4PM - 6PM US/Pacific
  • September 5, 2013 4:38:39 PM UTC-7
  • 4 pm US/Pacific on Sep 11 2013
  • About 10 miles

How to use it

Desktop browsers

Drag the link, l10nb9t, to the bookmark bar.

iOS mobile safari

  1. Bookmark this page.
  2. Copy the bookmarklet code:
  3. Edit the bookmark to alter the address with the bookmarklet code.

Known issues

  • We will add more date time formats and unit types.
  • Daylight saving time calculation available, but only configured for US/Pacific (PT)
  • Cannot convert date time representations among multiple DOM nodes
