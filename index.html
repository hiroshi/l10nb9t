l10nb9t - The localization bookmarklet

What?

Just click the "l10nb9t" link below. You may see date time representations in anohter time zones are converted to your local timezone (depend on your system settings).

Examples

  • Sep 4, 2013 18:00 UTC
  • Sep 10, 2013 10:00 PDT

How to use it

Desktop browsers

Drag the link, l10nb9t, to the bookmark bar.

iOS mobile safari

  1. Bookmark this page.
  2. Copy the bookmarklet link by long press.
  3. Edit the bookmark to alter the address with copied bookmarklet.

Known issues

  • Supported date time formats are restricted with the regexp in the script and your broswer's implementation of Date.parse() javscript function.
  • We will add more date time formats.
  • Convert units like "10 miles (16 km)"
  • Cannot convert date time representations among multiple nodes
